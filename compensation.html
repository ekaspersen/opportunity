2. Binary Commission: Your Rank Income

The core of the BE compensation plan operates on a Binary Structure, meaning you build two teams: a Left Team and a Right Team.

How it Works:

  • Paid Weekly: Commissions are paid out weekly.
  • Four-Week Cycles: Commissions are calculated in cycles that run for four weeks, Saturday to Saturday.
  • Build Only 2 Teams: Focus on your Left & Right teams.
  • Accumulating BV: You accumulate BV on both your left and right sides from personal sales, team sales, and even upline volume.

Your growth, your rewards!

Rank Advancement & Income:

By hitting specific BV targets on both sides within a cycle, you unlock different ranks and earn corresponding commissions.

Weekly Payouts Explained:

While ranks are based on four-week cycles, you get paid weekly! You receive 25% of your achieved rank's commission each week of the cycle. If you reach a higher rank mid-cycle, the system calculates previous payouts and pays the remaining balance of the higher rank's commission in the final week.

Example: Sapphire Rank ($1,500 for 4,000 BV L/R)

  • Week 1: Reach 1,200 BV L/R (Consultant level) → Earn $125 (25% of $500 Consultant commission).
  • Week 2: Continue growing, still at Consultant level → Earn another $125.
  • Week 3: Still at Consultant level → Earn another $125.
  • Week 4: Reach 4,000 BV L/R (Sapphire level). Total Sapphire commission is $1,500. You've already received $375. The system pays out the remaining $1,125 in this final week.

3. Generation Matching Bonus

This bonus allows you to earn a percentage of the rank commissions earned by leaders in your downline, extending beyond your direct enrollments.

How it Unlocks:

This bonus becomes available once you achieve the Emerald rank (29,500 BV L/R, earning $8,500 rank commission).

The key here is to help your team members achieve higher ranks, as their success directly contributes to your matching bonus!

Matching Bonus Tiers:

  • Emerald & Above: Earn 5% on the rank commission of the first Emeralds in all your enrollment lines.
  • Diamond & Above: Earn 5% on the rank commission of the first Diamonds (as well as Emeralds) in your enrollment lines.
  • Executive Diamond & Above: Earn 3% on the rank commission of the first Executive Diamonds in your enrollment lines.
  • Presidential Diamond & Above: Earn 3% on the rank commission of the first Presidential Diamonds in your enrollment lines.
  • Royal Diamond & Above: Earn 2% on the rank commission of the first Royal Diamonds in your enrollment lines.
